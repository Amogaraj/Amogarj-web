Product

Our flagship product, SmartBot 2.0, offers multilingual AI chatbot support for websites, WhatsApp, and Messenger. It's designed for easy use with drag-and-drop conversation builders. Businesses use it to reduce support costs and improve customer experience. The bot can take orders, book appointments, and handle FAQs automatically. It also integrates with CRMs like Zoho, Salesforce, and HubSpot. SmartBot uses natural language understanding to make conversations feel human. It also comes with reporting dashboards and live chat handoff. The system is hosted securely in the cloud and updates automatically. We also offer custom branding, voice recognition, and speech-to-text support. It’s mobile-friendly and supports multi-channel delivery.
